    Belly Biotics™: Gut Health Made Simple

    Our unique blend of pre, pro, and postbiotics helps to support a healthy gut, better digestion, and perfect poops.

    How It Works:

    Probiotics are living, beneficial microorganisms that help maintain balance in your dog’s gut. They assist with digestion, help crowd out harmful bacteria, and support immune health.

    Think of prebiotics as fertilizer for your dog’s gut microbiome. Prebiotics are specialized fibers that feed beneficial bacteria, helping them thrive. Without prebiotics, probiotics may pass through the digestive system without fully establishing themselves.

    Postbiotics are the beneficial compounds produced when probiotics break down prebiotics. They support the gut lining, reduce inflammation, and strengthen the immune system.

    Result:

    Less poop, firmer, and better-smelling.

    Because your dog can absorb more of the nutrients in our food, their body produces less waste.
